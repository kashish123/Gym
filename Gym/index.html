About Us

Welcome to Zero Gravity, our goal is to pro­vide you with the highest quality of fitness services to encourage an active and healthy lifestyle. ZG Fitness Center is designed by Physiotherapist doctor and an IIM management graduate. By enter­ing into an operating agreement with phsio, Fit­ness & Nutrition, we are able to provide a blend of high quality equipment and expert fitness profession­als to create a safe, convenient, and effective fitness environment. We are confident you will appreciate the value of having a high quality fitness facility at your disposal. Thank you for your valued trust on us.
